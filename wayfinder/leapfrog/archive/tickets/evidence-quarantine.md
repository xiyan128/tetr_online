## Question

Reset the campaign's evidence boundary before any repair can inherit a false
incumbent or authorizing result. Inventory round-0 variants and rounds 1-11,
classify each claim and artifact as valid, exploratory, superseded, or void,
and make promotion impossible from anything not explicitly valid. Round 11 may
finish as a diagnostic, but it cannot promote or seed the clean lineage.

The inventory must preserve useful forensic evidence without allowing the map,
round driver, or later reports to treat it as proof of compounding, purity, or
champion-relative strength.

## Acceptance evidence

- A linked, hash-bearing evidence index names every known model, corpus, duel,
  gate, and round receipt and gives its validity class plus reason.
- Rounds 1-10 and round 11's current run are mechanically ineligible for
  incumbent/lineage selection; a test demonstrates the refusal.
- The accepted-incumbent pointer is empty until the clean round-0 ticket closes.
- Every retracted map/ticket claim is listed with the exact replacement ticket;
  no artifact is deleted or silently relabeled.
- An executable audit command exits nonzero if any future manifest references a
  void or exploratory artifact as promotion evidence.
